Predicted autumn 2021 will be mainly cool and sunny. Days will gradually become shorter, which is typical for the season. Comparing to autumn 2020, autumn 2021 will be less dry. On average, heavy precipitation is not expected during the season. Average temperature of this fall 2021 - 55 °F which makes the season same as autumn 2020. Autumn's 2021 number of days with temperature over 57 °F will be 39 days, less then 54 °F - 31 days. Daylight hours on average will decrease down to 11 hours. The longest day will come on 1st September 2021 and last for 13 h 37 min. The shortest will be on 30th November 2021, its duration will be 8 h 6 min.
September 2021 will open the season classically
Long awaited first month of the autumn - seasons change time. September 2021 will show itself as typical autumn month. The first true typical for the season temperature is predicted on 8th September, which is later compared to September 2020. The average temperature will be 63 °F. By the way, previous September was similarly favorable. Average temperature of September 2020 fluctuated at 63 °F. Precipitation is predicted to be long-term on 4th - 24th September period.

October 2021 will be very autumn
Temperature background of the month will be high and predicted. Before 1st October the temperature will range from 45 °F to 59 °F. At the beginning of the month expected warm, dry weather. On some days the thermometer will go up to 68 °F, which nevertheless is higher compared to October 2020. Mid season appears to be with little precipitation, most of them will occur around 3rd - 29th October.
November 2021 will be warm
Average temperature November 2021 is 50 °F. The warmest day of the month will be on 2nd November with temperature of 61 °F, coldest day is on 26th November with average daily temperature around 37 °F. At the same time forecast for November 2021 is more favorable the same month of 2020 season. Precipitation in November will be heavy. According to the forecast the number of precipitation days is 12, mainly during the period 11th - 29th November.
